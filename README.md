# Supply Chain Risk & Logistics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyse global supply chain operations, logistics performance, and disruption risks. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor shipment activity, identify operational bottlenecks, evaluate disruption trends, and analyse geographic shipment distribution.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how business analysis and data visualisation can support data-driven decision-making within supply chain and logistics operations.

---

## Business Problem

Supply chains are increasingly exposed to disruptions caused by transportation issues, weather events, operational delays, and logistical inefficiencies. Organisations require visibility into shipment performance and risk factors to improve delivery reliability and operational efficiency.

This dashboard provides insights into:

* Shipment performance
* Supply chain disruptions
* Lead time trends
* Carrier reliability
* Geographic shipment distribution

## Dataset

The project uses a Global Supply Chain Risk & Logistics dataset containing shipment, transportation, disruption, weather, carrier, and geographic information.

Key fields include:

* Shipment ID
* Origin Port
* Destination Port
* Transport Mode
* Product Category
* Lead Time
* Carrier Reliability
* Weather Conditions
* Disruption Status
* Shipment Date

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of supply chain performance through:

* Total Shipments
* Disruption Rate
* Average Lead Time
* Carrier Reliability Score
* Disruptions by Transport Mode
* Lead Time by Product Category
* Disruptions by Weather Condition

---

### 2. Trend Analysis

Tracks disruption patterns over time through:

* Monthly Disruption Trends
* Peak Disruption Month
* Lowest Disruption Month
* Total Disruptions
* Change Percentage Analysis

This page helps identify seasonal trends and operational volatility.

---

### 3. Geographic Analysis

Provides location-based insights through:

* Global Shipment Distribution Map
* Origin Port Analysis
* Destination Port Analysis
* Port Performance Metrics

This page highlights key logistics hubs and shipment concentration patterns.

---

## Key Findings

### Overview Insights

* Air transport recorded the highest disruption volume.
* Hurricane conditions generated the highest weather-related disruption impact.
* Perishable products experienced the highest average lead times.

### Trend Insights

* Supply chain disruptions increased throughout the reporting period.
* November recorded the highest disruption volume.
* February recorded the lowest disruption volume.

### Geographic Insights

* Marseille was the busiest destination port.
* Rotterdam and Antwerp emerged as key European logistics hubs.
* Asian ports accounted for a significant share of shipment activity.
